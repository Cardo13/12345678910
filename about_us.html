About ZaiGlow

ZaiGlow was created from a place of passion, faith, and starting from nothing. I poured my heart into learning, growing and perfecting my craft so I could create a space where women feel cared for, comfortable and confident. Every client matters to me. I am committed to nail health, quality products, and exceptional care in every set. When you sit in my chair, you're not just another booking - you're supporting a dream and becoming part of my journey.
